Abstract Food for Thought

I am a firm believe that hard work beats talent when talent doesn’t work hard. If I had to choose a single skill that I felt was the most important to succeed, I would say nothing is more important than hard work. Sure you can be naturally skilled however if you are not applying 100% to that skill, it’s useless. If you work hard and put in the time and effort, you can accomplish so much more.
